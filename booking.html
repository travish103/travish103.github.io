Arriving

Taniti's airport can currently accommodate small-frame aircraft. The airport is currently being expanded.

You can also come in on the cruise ship that docks at Yellow Leaf Bay.

For your stay

We have a variety of places to stay on Taniti. Choose from any of our locally-owned hotels, or a bed and breakfast. There's even a four-star resort.

Getting around

Taniti City has public buses that run from 0500-2300 daily. The rest of the island is accessible via private bus routes.

Rental cars can be rented from the rental agency near the airport.

We have bikes and helmets (which are required by law) available for rent.

The cities of Taniti are also walkable!
