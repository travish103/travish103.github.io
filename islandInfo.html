Health

Here on Taniti, we have a Hospital with many multilingual employees, so in an emergency, we've got you covered.

Less urgent matters? Visit any one of our clinics across the island.

Food

We currently have 10 restaurants on the island. 5 of the restaurants serve fish (caught local!) and rice. 3 serve American-style meals, and 2 serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Looking to eat on a budget? Two supermarkets and two grocery stores are available for all your cooking needs.

Night-life

There are several pubs across Taniti, along with a microbrewery.

Looking to Dance? Visit our new dance club!
